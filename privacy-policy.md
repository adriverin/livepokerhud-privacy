# Live Poker HUD Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14.04.2026

## Overview
Live Poker HUD is a poker tracking and analysis app. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Storage
All poker-related data (players, sessions, hands, statistics) is stored locally on your device.

We do not upload or share this data with any server.

## Account Information
If you choose to create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Username

This information is used only for authentication purposes.

## Purchases
Premium subscriptions are handled by Apple via in-app purchases and RevenueCat.

We do not store or process payment information.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data.

## Third-Party Services
The app may use:
- Supabase (authentication)
- RevenueCat (subscription management)

These services may process limited account-related data.

## Your Control
You can:
- Use the app without an account
- Delete your data at any time within the app
- Delete your account via the app (if implemented)
